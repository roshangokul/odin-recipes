Recipe for French Fries

Description

This recipe will go over how to make the most delicious french fries you have ever had. With the most simplist of ingrediants, you can make this in no time!

Ingrediants

  • Potatoes
  • Cayanne Pepper
  • Oil
  • Kosher Salt

Steps

  1. Cut Potatoes
  2. Soak and Drain Potatoes
  3. Season the Fries
  4. Cook in Air Fryer
