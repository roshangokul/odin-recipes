Recipe for PB & J

Description

This recipe will go over how to make the most delicious peanut butter and jelly sandwich you have ever had. With the most simplist of ingrediants, you can make this in no time!

Ingrediants

  • Butter
  • White Bread
  • Grape Jelly
  • Peanut Butter

Steps

  1. Heat skillet to 350 degrees Farenheit
  2. Spread butter on one slice of bread. Then spread the jelly on the unbuttered side and peanut butter on the buttered side.
  3. Cook on skillet, four minutes on each side
